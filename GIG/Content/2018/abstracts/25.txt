Sprint Planning option within Tricentis Tosca will allow the Agile team to define the work and effort. 
Tricentis Tosca User can –
• Plan a Sprint by defining efforts at User Story and Resource level which help in defining a clear Sprint Goal
• Understand the Epic and User Story Status at any given point
• Understand the Resource Allocation Status (Resource Management)
• Evaluate Efforts

All Project artifacts are in or integrated within Tricentis Tosca
